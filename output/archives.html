Welcome To Glen Striders Running Club

Established on the principles of community spirit, Glen Striders is home to runners of all levels. Whether you're taking your first step or training for your tenth marathon, you'll find a supportive team to help you reach the finish line.

Join Us

Weekly Activities

Join us for our regular training sessions designed for runners of all levels. Each activity is crafted to help you improve your fitness and achieve your running goals.

Monday: Recovery Runs

Start your week with gentle recovery runs designed to help your body recover while maintaining your running routine. Perfect for all fitness levels.

Tuesday: 10K/5K Run

Challenge yourself with our Tuesday distance runs. Choose between 5K or 10K routes based on your current fitness level and goals.

Thursday: Time Trials

Push your limits with our Thursday time trials. Test your speed and track your progress in a supportive and competitive environment.

Saturday: Long Runs

Build your endurance with our Saturday long runs. Join the pack for extended distance training in a social and encouraging atmosphere.
