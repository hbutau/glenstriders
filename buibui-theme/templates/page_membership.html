Do I need to be fit to join?

No! We welcome runners of all fitness levels. Our training sessions cater to everyone from complete beginners to experienced athletes.

What equipment do I need?

Just a good pair of running shoes to start! As you progress, you may want to invest in moisture-wicking clothing and a running watch, but these aren't required initially.

Can I try before I join?

Absolutely! We encourage prospective members to join us for a training session to experience our club atmosphere before committing to membership.

Are there any other costs besides membership?

The annual membership fee covers your participation in all regular training sessions. Any special events, races, or optional club kit purchases would be additional.

What if I need to cancel my membership?

We understand circumstances change. Please contact a club committee member to discuss your situation.
