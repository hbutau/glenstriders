Meet Coach Itai Tembo (Coach Ittso)

Glen Striders is proud to welcome Coach Itai Tembo, affectionately known as Coach Ittso, as our Fitness and Conditioning Coach for 2026.

With over 25 years of experience in sports development, Coach Ittso brings a wealth of knowledge in athletic conditioning, training methodology, and performance optimization. His passion lies in nurturing athletes of all ages and abilities, helping them reach their full potential through structured, progressive training.

Background & Expertise

Coach Ittso's diverse sporting background spans multiple disciplines including soccer, netball, baseball, softball, tennis, athletics, and aerobics. This multi-sport experience gives him unique insights into cross-training principles, injury prevention, and building well-rounded athletic fitness—all essential elements for distance runners looking to improve their performance and longevity in the sport.

As a certified fitness and conditioning coach and sports official, he understands not just how to train athletes, but how to develop complete, resilient sportspeople who can perform consistently.

International Experience

Coach Ittso has represented Zimbabwe as an international baseball umpire at prestigious tournaments across Southern Africa, including:

  • U18 and Senior Ladies Baseball Championships (South Africa, 2024)
  • U18 Baseball World Cup Qualifiers (South Africa, 2025)
  • Senior Southern Africa Baseball5 Qualifiers (Lesotho)
  • U23 Men's Baseball World Cup Qualifiers (South Africa, 2025)
  • Senior African Championships (Zambia)
  • Ladies African Championships (Harare, 2025)

What This Means for Glen Striders

As your conditioning coach, Coach Ittso will focus on:

Strength & Conditioning

Tailored specifically for runners to build power and endurance

Injury Prevention

Through proper movement mechanics and core stability

Cross-Training Programs

To build overall athletic fitness and prevent burnout

Flexibility & Mobility

Work to improve running efficiency and range of motion

Progressive Training Plans

Suitable for runners at all levels, from beginners to advanced

Holistic Approach

Developing complete, well-rounded, resilient athletes

Ready to Take Your Running to the Next Level?

Whether you're training for your first 5K or chasing a marathon personal best, Coach Ittso's holistic approach to fitness will help you become a stronger, faster, and more resilient runner.
