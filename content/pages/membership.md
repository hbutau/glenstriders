## Join Glen Striders Running Club

### Membership Benefits

When you become a member of Glen Striders, you'll enjoy:

- **Structured Training Programs**: Access to professionally designed training plans for all levels
- **Group Training Sessions**: Join our regular Monday, Tuesday, Thursday, and Saturday runs
- **Expert Coaching**: Guidance from experienced coaches and seasoned runners
- **Social Events**: Be part of our running community through social gatherings and club events
- **Race Support**: Encouragement and support at local and regional races
- **Club Kit**: Access to purchase official Glen Striders running gear
- **Community Support**: Connect with like-minded runners who share your passion
- **Progress Tracking**: Monitor your improvement alongside supportive teammates

### Membership Fees

Our annual membership fees are designed to be affordable while supporting the club's activities:

- **Individual Membership**: $20 per year
- **Couples Membership**: $35 per year

All membership fees go towards:
- Organizing training sessions and events
- Coaching and training support
- Club administration and communication
- Social activities and awards

### Who Can Join?

Glen Striders welcomes runners of all abilities:

- **Beginners**: Never run before? No problem! We'll support you from your very first step
- **Recreational Runners**: Looking to stay fit and enjoy running in a social environment
- **Competitive Runners**: Training for your next personal best or podium finish
- **Marathon Enthusiasts**: Preparing for long-distance events with experienced training partners

### Weekly Training Schedule

As a member, you can participate in our regular training sessions:

- **Monday**: Recovery Runs - Gentle runs to start the week
- **Tuesday**: 10K/5K Runs - Distance training for building endurance
- **Thursday**: Time Trials - Speed work and performance testing
- **Saturday**: Long Runs - Extended distance training in a social setting

### How to Join

Ready to become part of the Glen Striders family? Here's how to join:

1. **Connect with Us**: Join our WhatsApp group or contact us through our social media channels
2. **Complete Membership Form**: Fill out our simple membership registration
3. **Pay Membership Fee**: Submit your annual membership fee
4. **Get Running**: Join us at any of our weekly training sessions

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I need to be fit to join?**  
No! We welcome runners of all fitness levels. Our training sessions cater to everyone from complete beginners to experienced athletes.

**What equipment do I need?**  
Just a good pair of running shoes to start! As you progress, you may want to invest in moisture-wicking clothing and a running watch, but these aren't required initially.

**Can I try before I join?**  
Absolutely! We encourage prospective members to join us for a training session to experience our club atmosphere before committing to membership.

**Are there any other costs besides membership?**  
The annual membership fee covers your participation in all regular training sessions. Any special events, races, or optional club kit purchases would be additional.

**What if I need to cancel my membership?**  
We understand circumstances change. Please contact a club committee member to discuss your situation.
