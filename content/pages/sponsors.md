## Sponsorship Program

The Glen Striders Running Club is a non-profit, all-volunteer organization. We count on the efforts of our members and community support to fulfill our mission to support runners in our area. There are exciting new changes to the club with a redesigned website and social media, new board, and new sponsorship program.

Glen Striders serves the running community in Glen View, Glen Norah, NBS, Fidelity, South View Park, Waterfalls, South Lea Park, and surrounding areas of Harare, Zimbabwe. We organize weekly training sessions, social runs, and community events that promote health, fitness, and camaraderie among runners of all levels.

### Ready to Sponsor the Club?

By sponsoring Glen Striders, you'll be supporting a vibrant running community while gaining visibility among health-conscious, active individuals in Harare. Our members represent diverse demographics and professions, providing excellent exposure for your business or brand.

### Sponsorship Levels

#### Starting Line – In Kind Donations, Gear, and Discounts

Help your business or brand reach new runners in our community and drive traffic to your business through:

- Coupons and discount codes for club members
- Product samples and freebies
- Services or merchandise offered to club members
- Gear donations for club events and giveaways

**Benefits**: Recognition on our website and social media, goodwill among club members, and direct access to a fitness-focused audience.

#### Bronze – Web Sponsor ($150 Yearly)

Reach running enthusiasts across Harare with digital presence:

- Logo placement on Glen Striders website
- Direct link to your business website
- Featured posts on Facebook
- Mentions on Strava club page
- Recognition on Instagram

**Benefits**: Year-round digital visibility, targeted audience reach, and enhanced brand recognition among active consumers.

#### Silver – Event Sponsor ($250 Yearly)

Be the official sponsor of a Glen Striders event and create memorable brand associations:

**Event Sponsorship Options:**
- Glen Striders Pizza Night
- Saturday Group Run
- Tuesday Pub Run
- Glen Striders Races

**Benefits**: Prominent logo placement at sponsored event, social media coverage before/during/after event, on-site brand visibility, and all Bronze tier benefits.

#### Gold – Title Sponsor ($500 Yearly or More)

Become a presenting sponsor of all Glen Striders races and events:

- Title sponsor recognition on all Glen Striders publications
- Prominent placement on website homepage
- Featured in all social media posts and announcements
- Logo on club communications and promotional materials
- VIP access to club events
- All benefits from Bronze and Silver tiers
- Custom sponsorship activation opportunities

**Benefits**: Maximum brand visibility, association with a respected community organization, extensive digital and physical presence, and priority partnership consideration for special initiatives.

### Why Sponsor Glen Striders?

- **Growing Community**: Join us as we expand running culture in Harare
- **Engaged Audience**: Our members are active, health-conscious individuals
- **Social Impact**: Support health and fitness in local communities
- **Brand Alignment**: Associate with positive values of determination, health, and community
- **Local Presence**: Connect with customers in Glen View, Glen Norah, NBS, Fidelity, South View Park, Waterfalls, South Lea Park, and broader Harare area
